Over or Under Payment

If an overpayment occurs, the over paid amount will be refunded after staff approval. Partial or underpayments will not be processed until the full statement amount is received.

Bank Account Changes

If you have changed your bank account name or decide to use a different bank account, please inform BCROSRev@gov.bc.ca of your new short name.
